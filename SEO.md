**Pages:** 133 &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Language:** zh-CN primary / en secondary

**Content distribution:**
| Section | Pages | SEO role |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Knowledge base | 55 | Long-tail authority content |

**Title format:** `Chinese keyword｜sub-topic · City · City` — descriptive, keyword-rich  
**Example:** `湾区卖房全流程｜从准备到上市到交割`

**Strengths:**
- Knowledge base is the biggest SEO asset — 55 long-form articles targeting niche queries like "pre-IPO stock to luxury home", "Prop 19 property tax transfer", "K-shaped housing recovery 2025"
- City-specific sell pages capture high-intent local queries in 13 cities
- Case studies signal E-E-A-T (experience, expertise, authoritativeness, trustworthiness) — Google's primary trust signal for YMYL (financial/real estate) content
- Bilingual titles help capture both `湾区卖房` and `bay area sell home` search intents

**Weaknesses:**
- URL slugs are English-only (`/knowledge/bay-area-market-q1-2026`) while titles are Chinese — mixed signals for zh-CN search targeting
- No hreflang tags to separate zh-CN and en audiences
- No schema markup (LocalBusiness, RealEstateAgent) — missed structured data opportunity
- `/en/*` subdirectory excluded entirely — no English-only SEO surface

## Cross-Site SEO Observations

### Funnel Architecture
The three sites form a logical buyer journey but it's not explicitly connected:
```
bayareaschoolguide.com   →   mkbayarea.com / estatesmk.com
(School research)             (Buy/sell real estate)
```
Internal cross-linking between sites would strengthen the funnel and pass PageRank across the brand.

### Bilingual Targeting
All three sites mix zh-CN titles with English URL slugs. This is a common pattern for targeting diaspora Chinese audiences on Google (vs. Baidu), but creates inconsistency:
- Google may classify pages as `zh-CN` based on content but `en` based on URL
- Adding `hreflang="zh-Hans"` meta tags would resolve the ambiguity

### Content Freshness
- Market data pages (`market-q1-2026`, etc.) will decay quickly — these need quarterly updates to maintain rankings
- School pages are semi-evergreen but test scores and feeder patterns change annually
- Knowledge articles targeting timeless topics ("1031 exchange", "trust vs LLC") are the most durable SEO assets

### Missing Schema Markup
None of the three sites appear to use structured data. High-impact opportunities:
| Site | Schema type | Benefit |
|------|-------------|---------|
| mkbayarea.com | `RealEstateAgent`, `LocalBusiness` | Rich snippets, Google Business integration |
| estatesmk.com | `RealEstateAgent`, `Product` (listings) | Trust signals |
| bayareaschoolguide.com | `School`, `EducationalOrganization` | Knowledge panel eligibility |
